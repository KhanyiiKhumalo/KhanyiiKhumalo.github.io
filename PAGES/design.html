IxD Process

IxD Process

My IxD documentation
Goal Alignments:
My aim is to make it easy for the user (the marker) to browse around my portfolio website, which will have a homepage, a blog part, a design section, an essay section, a profile screen, and a portfolio area. Also my goal is to make my website look aesthetically pleasing and display my personality and interests.

Information Structure (Content Mapping):
Each page has a navigation bar at the top of the screen that is fixed. The homepage (the first page of the website) provides a quick overview of the website and its navigation.All my pages have a similar format with the nav-bar at the top, the name of that section as well as a short description of that section. The only difference will be at the bottom of the pages when you scroll down that will display the content of that page in different ways. The Blog page includes a blog selection window as well as the actual blogs. The Design page will include a style guide, wireframes, and an explanation of my IxD approach (much like this section of the blog). There will be nothing in the essay area at this time. Similar to the blog page, the Portfolio page will have placeholders for my future portfolio pages to be added.

User Flow (Screens, Behavior, and Decisions):
The user will begin at the homepage. They will select one of the four sections at the top of the page to go to. If the user navigates to the Blog or Portfolio pages, they may utilize the corresponding selection panel to skip to the chosen blog article or portfolio item. Once the user has selected a blog post or project they can click on ‘Read More’ or ‘View Project’ that will take them a new page to either read or view that item. When a visitor visits the Design or Essay sections they can scroll down to see the essay or utilize the image expand function to see the wireframes and style guides. Sections like the Essay and Portfolio have the work displayed and one can just rread the essays and click on the links for the portfolios to display the project.

Interface Elements:
The user may interact with my website in two ways: by scrolling through each page or by clicking on links that will move the user elsewhere on that page or to another page.

Design (Style Guide, Wireframes):
The website layout incorporates a clean and modern design, predominantly using the colors white, grey, black, and dusty pink. The overall aesthetic is elegant and sophisticated, creating a visually pleasing and user-friendly experience. Fonts play a crucial role in the website's visual appeal and readability. The header text and prominent titles feature the Playfair Display font, which adds a touch of sophistication and elegance to the design. For the body text and smaller headings, the Roboto font is used, providing clarity and legibility. Quicksand is utilized sparingly for accent text or to add variety and visual interest. Additionally, serif fonts are used for block quotes or longer excerpts to create a sense of distinction. Readability and legibility are prioritized throughout the layout, ensuring that users can easily consume the content. Ample line spacing, clear font sizes, and appropriate font weights are implemented to optimize legibility, particularly for longer paragraphs. The color palette consists primarily of white, which creates a sense of cleanliness and openness. Grey is employed as a neutral background color, adding depth and visual contrast. Black is used selectively to create emphasis or for elements requiring high contrast. Dusty pink is employed as an accent color, adding a subtle hint of warmth and femininity to the overall design. Color theory and relationships are considered when applying the color palette. The dusty pink color complements the neutral tones of white, grey, and black, creating a harmonious and cohesive visual experience. The use of pink sparingly throughout the layout allows it to stand out and draw attention to important elements, such as buttons or call-to-action sections. Composition is carefully crafted to achieve balance and harmony. The layout utilizes a grid system, ensuring elements are aligned and organized in a visually pleasing manner. Negative space is incorporated to provide breathing room and enhance readability. The hierarchy of information is established through variations in font size, weight, and positioning, enabling users to navigate and understand the content intuitively. Interface elements are designed to be visually appealing and user-friendly. Buttons are sleek and minimalist, utilizing the dusty pink color for hover or active states to indicate interactivity. Icons and graphical elements follow a consistent design style, employing clean lines and simple shapes to enhance clarity and ease of recognition. Shape, color, and position are utilized to create visual interest and guide user attention. Geometric shapes with rounded corners are employed for section dividers or decorative elements, adding a modern touch to the layout. The dusty pink color is strategically placed to draw focus to important areas or call-to-action buttons. Key elements are positioned strategically to create a sense of flow and guide users through the content intuitively. Overall, the website layout combines the elegance of Playfair Display with the clarity of Roboto and Quicksand, while utilizing a color palette of white, grey, black, and dusty pink to create a visually pleasing and user-friendly experience. The design elements work harmoniously to ensure readability, balance, and visual appeal, enhancing the overall user experience on the website.
