    Your experience setting up Git.
    The setup of Github was easy and seamless but a bit confusing if you didn’t know where to look. As someone who has never used Github, I didn’t know where to look so signup because it was so small and tucked away in the corner of the screen. But once you are set up and an interesting display of the galaxy takes over your screen it just plays around with the application and tries to figure it out.

    Reflection on the readings.
    The 3 readings I read were informative but I found the terminology confusing and got lost at some stages, to the point where I would read a sentence over again to try and understand. After doing some research on the internet about the readings and their perspective authors then only did I start to understand more. I think I could look more into the readings in the future and possibly ask others for their feedback and how they understood the readings.
